**Fascinated by their design, I have always kept a few tokens from the arcades I used to visit without spending them.** However, I began systematically collecting them only in the early nineties when it became clear that most of these arcades would soon disappear.

Most of these little coins tell their own story – the story of a bar or an arcade where people used to have fun playing arcade games, places that no longer exist today.

These tokens come from my personal collection, gathered in Italian bars and arcades during the eighties and nineties.
