## 8BitDo and Amazon Fire Stick / Fire Cube

After testing them, I wanted to confirm that the new 8BitDo Neo-Geo controllers **work perfectly** with Amazon Fire Stick and Fire Cube when using RetroArch in Bluetooth mode.

## Support on MacOS

**On my Intel-based Mac with macOS 12.7, they are causing me a lot of problems.** Whether in Bluetooth mode or using the dongle, the controller doesn't seem to be recognized 100%, and unfortunately, at the time of writing this article, the latest version of the update tool (2.4.1) no longer works on my Mac because they have removed support for Intel processors.
