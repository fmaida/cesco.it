My name is Francesco Maida, and I am a 46-year-old computer technician living in Venice. For the past eight years, I've been helping my parents run our family restaurant. However, due to COVID-19, we are currently all unemployed and struggling financially. As a result, I am currently looking for temporary or project-based employment.

## Developed Skills

Here are the skills I have developed over the years:

### PHP

In the past, I have developed several WordPress websites and a couple of simple plugins for it. Specifically, I created these plugins to enable multilingual websites at a time when there were no specific professional plugins available for this purpose. In the same field, I later worked as a user on the first Italian translation of WPML. For the university's language center in Venice, I created a PHP-based seat reservation system for classrooms (along with a small management system in Visual Basic that allowed the administration to open and close availability days).

Many years ago, I developed a real estate website for Gabetti's Venice branch, consisting of a PHP server and a Visual Basic client for uploading photos and descriptions. I also created a couple of small, simple CMSs purely for amateur use for some websites I maintained at the time.

### Python

I have studied Python, Flask, and Django at an amateur level, creating some simple utilities for my work as an accountant and receptionist in the family restaurant. For example, one of the small utilities I created allowed me to extract a raw list of tour guides visiting Venice in a particular week and organize it based on the day and the guide's availability to bring clients to our restaurant. Another utility allowed me to enter a series of invoices to generate an installment payment plan for the invoice balance.

With Flask, I created a simple Alexa skill that provided information about tide levels and possible "high water" events in Venice. The skill was essentially a combination of a Python script that periodically retrieved updated information from the Venice tide center and a Flask website that managed a REST endpoint to respond to user requests.

### Filemaker

I developed several internal management systems for the family restaurant, including a warehouse inventory management system with inventory loading and unloading, a customer reservation system, and one for managing reservations for tourist groups in the dining area.

### HTML/CSS

Over the years, I have created several static websites in addition to some WordPress sites. Besides my personal website created with Hugo, my latest project was the website for my parents' restaurant, written in Pug/Jade (a preprocessor I have preferred to direct HTML writing for some years). I purchased a graphic template from a website and then converted it from HTML to Pug, first creating a working template and then implementing various translations in Italian, English, and Russian by extending this template with text in the respective languages. Using Netlify as the platform, I added the management of a simple form for receiving requests and reservations.

### JavaScript

Apart from a basic study of Vue.js and Angular 1.5, I have not delved deeply into important frameworks. I can write some simple routines with jQuery or vue.js without much difficulty, but I cannot consider myself an expert in the field.

In the past, I wrote some simple programs in JavaScript and TypeScript for personal enjoyment, including one that used HTML5 audio libraries and an external library to generate an audio file in .WAV format from another type of document.

### Other Skills

I also created a couple of small management programs in Visual Basic for the university's language center in Venice, including one that allowed the administration to manage enrolled students. For Fioreria Fantin in Venice, I developed a small invoicing program capable of archiving, printing, and exporting invoices in PDF format.
